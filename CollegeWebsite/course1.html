List of Courses offered at Graduate level

Engineering
An undergraduate engineering degree programme B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology) in Imperial University of Higher Education,is a 4 years full time programme in a specific branch of engineering (i.e. Major degree). Students are required to earn approximately 160 credits spread over these 4 years in any one of the following Major programmes:
  1. Computer Science (B Tech)
  2. Information Technology (B Tech)
Who Can Apply
  • An Indian student who has passed 10+2 examination in Science with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects
  • NRI / Foreign Nationals / Children of Gulf workers/ OCI students

Management
Imperial University of Higher Education also offers an undergraduate degree in Business Management and Finance. This is a 3 years full time programme either in the field of finance.   2. Finance and Accounting (BFA)

Academic Flexibility

Students enrolled in any of the above major programmes offered by the college can earn additional credits to acquire

  • Honours: if in the same branch as Major so as to deep dive in the subject
  • Minor: if in some other branch of the college or in other fields offered by colleges on campus so as to broaden their horizons
  • Dual degree:5 year dual degree program (Tech + Management):Students can accelerate their career path with this dual degree.
  • Avail semester long internship in industry or research organizations
